Service & Warranty Policy

Overview

Baladi Hypermarket stands behind the quality of every product we sell. This Service & Warranty Policy outlines the coverage, terms, and process for warranty claims and product servicing.

Manufacturer Warranty

Most products sold by Baladi Hypermarket come with a manufacturer's warranty. The duration and terms of manufacturer warranties vary by brand and product type:

Product Category Typical Manufacturer Warranty
Smartphones & Tablets 12 months
Laptops & Computers 12–24 months
Large Home Appliances 12–24 months
Small Appliances 6–12 months
Audio / Visual Equipment 12 months
Accessories & Peripherals 3–6 months

Note: Always refer to the warranty card included in the product box for the exact terms applicable to your specific product.

What the Manufacturer Warranty Covers

Standard manufacturer warranties typically cover:

  • Manufacturing defects — faults in materials or workmanship that exist at the time of purchase.
  • Component failures — parts that stop functioning under normal operating conditions.
  • Hardware malfunctions not caused by external factors.

What the Manufacturer Warranty Does NOT Cover

The following are generally excluded from manufacturer warranties:

  • Physical damage caused by accidents, drops, or impact.
  • Liquid damage or exposure to moisture.
  • Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications.
  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, dents, battery degradation over time).
  • Damage caused by incorrect use contrary to the user manual.
  • Products with removed or altered serial numbers or warranty stickers.
  • Consumable parts (such as batteries after significant charge cycles, printer ink).
  • Software issues, viruses, or data loss.

How to Make a Warranty Claim

  • Contact Support / Visit Service Center Contact Baladi Hypermarket Customer Support or visit any of our authorized service centers.
  • Present Proof of Purchase Present your original proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation email).
  • Initial Diagnosis Describe the issue clearly. Our technician will perform an initial diagnosis.
  • Repair or Replacement If the defect is confirmed as a warranty-covered issue, we will proceed with repair or replacement at no charge.
  • Timeline Estimate If parts need to be ordered, we will provide an estimated repair timeline.
  • Collection & Delivery You will be notified once your product is ready for collection or delivery.

Repair Timelines

  • Minor repairs: Two to five business days.
  • Major repairs / part orders: Seven to fifteen business days.
  • Complex cases requiring manufacturer involvement: Up to thirty business days.
  • We will keep you informed throughout the process.

Out-of-Warranty Repairs

If your product is outside the warranty period or the damage is not covered by warranty, we can still provide paid repair services through our authorized service centers. A repair estimate will be provided before any work begins, and you may accept or decline.

Loan Devices

For products that require extended repair periods (exceeding seven business days), Baladi Hypermarket may offer a temporary loan device subject to availability and the nature of the product. Loan devices must be returned in the same condition they were issued.

Data Responsibility

Baladi Hypermarket is not responsible for any data loss that occurs during the repair process. We strongly advise customers to back up all personal data before submitting a device for service.

Customer Obligations

  • Handle products in accordance with the manufacturer's guidelines.
  • Do not attempt to repair, open, or modify the product yourself unless explicitly permitted.
  • Keep your proof of purchase in a safe place for the duration of the warranty period.
  • Report defects promptly — delayed reporting may complicate warranty assessments.
