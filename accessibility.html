Website Accessibility Policy

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Baladi Hypermarket is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to our products, information, and services.

We strive to meet the standards set by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA, which is internationally recognized as the benchmark for digital accessibility.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to improve accessibility across our website:

Visual Accessibility

  • High contrast color schemes for improved readability.
  • Scalable text that can be enlarged without loss of content or functionality.
  • Descriptive alt text for all meaningful images and graphics.
  • Clear visual focus indicators for keyboard navigation.

Navigation Accessibility

  • Full keyboard navigability without requiring a computer mouse.
  • Skip navigation links to allow users to jump directly to main content.
  • Consistent and predictable page layouts across all sections.
  • Breadcrumb navigation for easy user orientation.

Content Accessibility

  • Clear, plain language used throughout all site copy.
  • Structured headings (H1, H2, H3) optimized for screen reader compatibility.
  • Descriptive link text (avoiding phrases like "click here" or "read more" without appropriate context).
  • Captions and transcripts for video and audio content where applicable.

Forms & Interactions

  • Clearly labeled form fields with helpful real-time error messages.
  • Sufficient time allowed for completing time-sensitive interactions.
  • Elimination of content that flashes more than three times per second to prevent seizure risks.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (such as JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver).
  • Screen magnification software.
  • Voice recognition software (such as Dragon NaturallySpeaking).
  • Keyboard-only navigation.
  • Alternative input devices.

Known Limitations

While we work continuously to improve accessibility, some areas of our website may not yet be fully compliant. These include:

  • Certain third-party embedded content (such as external payment portals and social media widgets) over which we have limited control.
  • Some older PDF documents that are in the process of being updated.

We are actively working to resolve these limitations.

Feedback & Reporting Issues

We welcome feedback from users who encounter accessibility barriers. If you experience any difficulty accessing our website or any content:

  • Email: accessibility@baladihappiness.com
  • Phone: Available during business hours
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within three business days

Please describe the issue, the page where you encountered it, and the assistive technology or browser you are using. We take all feedback seriously and will work to address issues promptly.

Third-Party Content

We cannot guarantee the accessibility of content hosted on third-party websites linked from our site. We encourage users to review the accessibility policies of those websites directly.

Policy Review

This accessibility policy is reviewed annually and updated to reflect improvements made to our website.
